Express Management System (EMS)

The Express Management System is a web-based platform developed using Angular, designed to efficiently manage various aspects of the shipping process and provide a comprehensive suite of services to meet the needs of businesses and individuals. This system covers a range of services and products, including:
  • Cross Border e-commerce: Seamlessly handle cross-border ecommerce operations, ensuring smooth international transactions.
  • Express: Deliver packages of all sizes with speed and reliability. The system stands out with its professional services and advanced technological features.
  • Freight Forwarding: Manage international airfreight and ocean freight operations, including handling general, dangerous goods (DG), and project cargo.
  • Customs Brokerage: Utilize comprehensive knowledge of customs regulations to facilitate import and export customs clearance. The system also provides regulatory support, compliance assistance, and consulting services.
  • Transportation And Distribution: Utilize an extensive logistics network and cutting-edge technology to efficiently distribute goods across strategic locations.
  • FMX Ecommerce Fulfillment (FeF): Leverage the Express Link Logistics business model to enhance performance control, security, and tailored operational solutions. This includes kitting, assembly, and value-added services.
  • Value Added Services: Offer additional services such as Cash On Delivery (COD), return management, bulk shipment services, reverse pickup, time-critical delivery, and more for domestic shipments.

Express Services

The system provides a wide range of express delivery services, including:
  • Domestic Express: Guaranteed delivery commitments within specific timeframes for main towns, outskirts, and rural areas.
  • Domestic Economy Express: Economical express services with slightly longer delivery commitments for main towns and outskirts.
  • International Express: Reliable international delivery to over 200 countries within agreed transit times.
  • International Economy Express: Cost-effective international delivery for less urgent consignments within specified weight limits.
  • Same Day Delivery: Collection and delivery on the same day for items picked up before 1 PM.

Freight Forwarding Services

The system offers comprehensive freight forwarding services, including international airfreight and ocean freight, catering to both full container load (FCL) and less than container load (LCL) shipments.

Customs Brokerage Services

With deep understanding of customs regulations, the system provides import and export customs clearance services, regulatory support, compliance assistance, consulting, foreign trade zone management, duty drawback, and customs bond handling.

Transportation and Distribution

Leveraging an extensive logistics network and advanced technology, the system offers full truck load (FTL) and less than truck load (LTL) services. Additionally, it facilitates cross-border transportation between Malaysia, Singapore, and Thailand.

FMX Ecommerce Fulfillment (FeF)

The system's business model ensures superior control, customizable solutions, and operational security for ecommerce fulfillment. It offers temperature-controlled environments, kitting, assembly, and other value-added services.

Value Added Services

For domestic shipments, the system provides a range of value-added services, including Cash On Delivery (COD), return management, bulk shipment services, reverse pickup, time-critical delivery, and regular/late pickup options. In summary, the Express Management System, built on Angular, is a versatile and advanced platform designed to streamline shipping processes, offer a comprehensive suite of services, and cater to the diverse needs of businesses and customers in the shipping industry.
